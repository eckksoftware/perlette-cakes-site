# Brand Notes

Working copy and brand-direction notes captured from the business owner. This file is internal reference material and can be refined later as homepage and about-page copy evolve.

## Brand Motto

`Made With Love, Made With Care`

## Positioning Notes

Perlette Cakes is a home-based bake shop built on the motto: Made With Love, Made With Care. Every baked good is prepared with the same care and attention that would be given to the baker's own celebrations.

Voice is still being decided between:
1. `we / our`
2. `I / my`

Current leaning from project decisions:
1. Keep the business feeling personal and home-based
2. Favor first-person copy in the owner story where appropriate
3. Keep factual business statements clear and neutral where SEO/LLM clarity matters most

## Owner-Provided Copy Fragments

These are source notes, not final published copy.

### Delicious-first philosophy

Our passion lies in making absolutely delicious cakes that would be finished at every event. Cakes that guests happily go back for, cakes that are finished at the table and cakes that you share with your loved ones in the refrigerator light while your pets roam at your feet.

For us, cakes are not just about creating something beautiful. It's about creating something absolutely delicious - the kind of cake that gets enjoyed until the very last slice, the kind that guests remember, and the kind that makes its way into conversations even after the celebration ends.

### Premium ingredient and value positioning

Some customers may find the prices to be above average, but that is because Perlette Cakes does not skimp on ingredients or quality. The products sold are based on the same recipes used for friends' and family's birthdays and events.

The owner wants the brand to communicate that buying from a home baker can mean better care, strong portions, premium ingredients, and no filler ingredients or cheaper substitutions used only to improve margins.

### Trust signals to preserve in future copy

1. Homemade, but premium
2. Beautiful and delicious, with taste as a priority
3. Suitable for real celebrations and shared moments
4. Quality ingredients with no corner-cutting
5. Personal care from a home-based baker

## Copy Constraints

1. Do not publish unapproved pricing/value language on the stage-one website
2. Do not present testimonial claims without real customer quotes
3. Keep the final public copy aligned with a home-based business expectation
4. Revisit pronoun choice before finalizing the owner story and homepage copy
